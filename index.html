About PLT

We’ve been building unique metal art pieces, furniture, and creations.

“Perfection is not attainable, but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence.”-Vince Lombardi

I am Mr. Pedro. I have worked in metal fabrication, for over 15 years. I specialize in creating detailed custom pieces of metal, as well as a variety of metal repair work. I do take in custom metal art request. I pride myself on making pieces that last. No matter the task I make sure that I carefully craft each project according to the outlined specifications so that it's done right the first time. My process to create majestic pieces of metal art is simple. I like to start with broad concepts and as I start forming them, they evolve into something extraordinary. If you want EXCELLENT pieces of metal art to compliment your home, workspace, etc. PLT is here to create that just for you.
